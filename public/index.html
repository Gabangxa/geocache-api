How it works

  
Three steps. One smart cache layer between you and expensive geocoding APIs.

  

    

      
01

      
Send your address

      
POST to /v1/geocode with your address string. Include your API key in the header.

    

    

      
02

      
Cache checked first

      
GeoCache checks its distributed cache. If the address was seen in the last 24 hours, it returns instantly at zero credit cost.

    

    

      
03

      
Only pay for misses

      
Cache misses hit the upstream geocoding provider and consume one credit. The result is cached for all future callers.

    

    

      
04

      
Track your savings

      
GET /v1/usage shows hit rate, credits used, and estimated USD saved from cache hits.
